About Us
We’re a group of creative thinkers who have built a modpack to change your Minecraft experience.

Creating a large modpack is already proved to be a very hard feat to accomplish. One may experience many tough challenges, such as the numerous amount of crash reports you would receive, the hundreds of glitches you will encounter, and the lag you may receive. However, one person PulseBeat_02, with another developer BomberCost, has decided to start building to this dream. Today, we present to you “King Mammoth”, the world’s largest modpack inspired by other famous modpacks out there.

You may ask, how did you make this modpack run so fast? How did you make such a large modpack that doesn’t crash? These questions are still not yet answerable by the King Mammoth Team. Although we have not found an answer to these questions, we have strived and pushed our limits to optimize this modpack as far as we can, whether it is from using the complex mechanics of Java to manipulate the memory use, to just removing unnecessary mods from the modpack.

Around 2018 and 2019, we released our first copy of the modpack. It was very buggy and proved to not be a success. Many complained from the unbearable lag they would receive in their clients, the chunk corruptions in their single-player worlds, and so much more. We decided to address these problems immediately and improve our modpack for future updates. Today, King Mammoth is a thriving community with a large number of members in the discord.

We have almost hit 1000 downloads, and we hope that the community will grow larger as time passes.

- PulseBeat_02, Founder of King Mammoth.

How Do We Plan It All Out?

Our Services

Support
Support is generally given usually by emailing us, or by telling us on the discord. If possible, please post all crash reports and logs so we can diagnose the issue and fix the bug in our modpack.
Strategy
What made King Mammoth such a successful pack in the beginning was all the planning involved before. This includes writing an organizer about mods, configs, and much more to create our modpack.
Management
We manage our modpack as much as we can, depending on the time. Due to education purposes, development will be greatly slowed down, but releases and updates will still be done.
Events
We release our modpack on Technic, Minecraft Forum, and on our discord. Patch-Lists will be given out on the release dates.
Applying
We still have many people joining our community, and some of them, want to take part as a role in this modpack. In order to take part in this modpack, you must read all rules carefully, contribute, and a short interview if necessary.
Consulting
Generally, we consult with all of our developers to produce a fix for a bug that was passed down. This allows us to be very quick when it comes to fixing problems.
