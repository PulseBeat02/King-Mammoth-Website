About Us
We’re a group of creative thinkers who have built a modpack to change your Minecraft experience.

Creating the world's largest modpack was once a dream. From facing tough challenges,
such as numerous crash reports, hundreds of glitches, tons of lag fixes, we have
finally made it. Based on inspiration from other modpacks, we have decided
to give our users the "King Mammoth Experience" , by giving them
a chance to experience the result of all the hard work.

What were the results? Well, many of the users started off with complementing
us with how the structure of our modpack is setup very well.
Sometimes, this would include selection of mods,
configs, and scripts together to make
this modpack possible.

Today, we have made a far leap from our first release. Before, our modpack
was not very popular, and did not hit a ton of downloads. Today,
King Mammoth has hit over 1000 runs on Technic,
and over 280 installs. We hope that
our community continues to
grow and strive as time
passes,

Our Services

Support
Support is generally given usually by emailing us, or by telling us on the discord. If possible, please post all crash reports and logs so we can diagnose the issue and fix the bug in our modpack.
Strategy
What made King Mammoth such a successful pack in the beginning was all the planning involved before. This includes writing an organizer about mods, configs, and much more to create our modpack.
Management
We manage our modpack as much as we can, depending on the time. Due to education purposes, development will be greatly slowed down, but releases and updates will still be done.
Events
We release our modpack on Technic, Minecraft Forum, and on our discord. Path-Lists will be given out on the release dates.
Applying
We still have many people joining our community, and some of them, want to take part as a role in this modpack. In order to take part in this modpack, you must read all rules carefully, contribute, and a short interview if necessary.
Consulting
Generally, we consult with all of our developers to produce a fix for a bug that was passed down. This allows us to be very quick when it comes to fixing problems.
